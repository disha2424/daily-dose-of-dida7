WHO'S WATCHING

MOVIE RECOMENDATION

This is a list of movie recommendations that I personally consider the best in their respective genres. I'm passionate about movies, and I appreciate every aspect, from the storytelling and acting to the cinematography.
You'll find a number of Bollywood movies in this list, and I believe that Bollywood, with its dramatic dialogue delivery, singing, and dancing, forms a unique genre of its own. Most Bollywood films tend to fall into the categories of romance or action. While this might seem repetitive to some, it's what the Indian audience is accustomed to. If you're new to Bollywood cinema, it's essential to approach it with an open mind, as these movies can sometimes be a bit nonsensical. Bollywood has a specific style that may not appeal to everyone, but it captivates about 90% of Indians, myself included. I would recommend Bollywood movies to anyone interested in exploring this distinct cinematic experience.

BOLLYWOOD

YEH JAWANI HAI DIWANI

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" struck a chord with teenagers upon its release, becoming a cultural phenomenon. My friends and I could recite it word for word. Despite its basic storyline, it resonated with the youth of India like no other film. It's a perfect 10/10 for me, as it's a unique cinematic gem that captured the essence of young adulthood like never before.

JANE TU...YA JANE NA

"Jaane Tu Ya Jaane Na" is a cherished classic that captured my heart at the tender age of 4, making me dance in the theater to one of its songs. Even at 19, the movie's magic still compels me to dance in my seat. In simple terms, it's a heartwarming ode to friendship, boasting an engaging storyline. It inspires you to give your friends a warm hug or, as I did at the age of 4, wish for friends like those on screen.

KUCH KUCH HOTA HAU

"Kuch Kuch Hota Hai" is a true Bollywood classic that introduced many over-the-top dramatic tropes to the genre. The story is beautiful, but the acting steals the show. The film features a 10-year time jump, and Kajol's portrayal of the female lead undergoing a significant character transformation is simply stunning. Even after countless viewings, it feels like two different actors are playing the role. Her transformation is not just in her appearance but also in her mannerisms, speech, and demeanor, conveying a sense of maturity and calmness. This movie is a definite must-watch for everyone.

KABHI KHUSHI KABHIE GHAM

"Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham" is another Bollywood classic that hardly needs an introduction. This film's legendary dialogues continue to be reminisced in other movies even today. Its iconic status in Indian cinema is well-deserved, making it a timeless favorite that has left an indelible mark on the hearts of movie enthusiasts.

KHOOBSURAT

"Khoobsurat," a 2014 gem, is often overlooked but shouldn't be. Sonam Kapoor shines as Dr. Milli Chakravarty, a bubbly physiotherapist treating a royal family patriarch. Her performance is both hilarious and endearing, showcasing her acting talent. Fawad Khan impresses in his debut as the disciplined Vikram Rathore. With strong supporting roles from Ratna Pathak and Kirron Kher, "Khoobsurat" offers a delightful mix of humor and romance, making it a perfect choice for a date night. Despite being overshadowed by other 2014 releases, this film deserves recognition as a top 10 contender. Don't miss out on this hidden gem.

STUDENT OF THE YEAR

"Student of the Year," directed by Karan Johar, may appear as a typical Bollywood film with its affluent protagonists and opulent backdrop, but it transcends those expectations. It's a one-of-a-kind masterpiece that delves into the lives of three students at St. Teresa College. With a young and talented cast featuring Alia Bhatt, Varun Dhawan, and Sidharth Malhotra, the movie explores friendship, competition, and dreams. It offers a visual feast with memorable music and goes beyond the surface to provide a deeper, more meaningful experience. "Student of the Year" is a unique gem that defies clichÃ©s and is a must-watch for Bollywood enthusiasts.

ROM-COMS

PRETTY WOMAN

"Pretty Woman" is a timeless romantic classic that pairs Julia Roberts and Richard Gere in an unlikely love story. Roberts shines as a prostitute who captures the heart of a wealthy businessman. The film beautifully balances romance and comedy, making it an enduring favorite. With its memorable quotes and iconic moments, "Pretty Woman" remains a heartwarming tale of love transcending social boundaries.

HOW TO LOOSE A GUY IN 10 DAYS

"How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days" is a hilarious rom-com starring Kate Hudson and Matthew McConaughey. Hudson plays a writer trying to drive her date away in just 10 days as part of an article, while McConaughey portrays a man trying to make her fall in love. Their comedic chemistry and witty antics create an entertaining and memorable love story.

CLUELESS

"Clueless" is a cult classic, a modern-day adaptation of Jane Austen's "Emma" set in a Beverly Hills high school. Alicia Silverstone shines as the fashionable and charming Cher, navigating teenage life and matchmaking with humor and style.

10 THINGS I HATE ABOUT YOU

"10 Things I Hate About You" is a delightful teen rom-com inspired by Shakespeare's "The Taming of the Shrew." It features an ensemble cast led by Heath Ledger and Julia Stiles. The film is a perfect blend of wit, humor, and heart.

FRIENDS WITH BENIFITS

"Friends with Benefits" is a contemporary take on the friends-to-lovers trope, starring Mila Kunis and Justin Timberlake. The film explores the complexities of modern relationships with humor and authenticity.

NOTTING HILL

"Notting Hill" pairs Hugh Grant and Julia Roberts in a charming romantic story. Grant plays a British bookseller who falls in love with a famous American actress, played by Roberts. The film is a heartwarming exploration of love, fame, and the collision of two different worlds.

COMING OF AGE

EASY A

"Easy A" is a witty high school comedy starring Emma Stone, who pretends to be promiscuous to boost her social status. The film cleverly explores themes of gossip, reputation, and peer pressure, all with a dose of humor. Emma Stone's charismatic performance and the film's clever dialogue make it a standout in the teen comedy genre. "Easy A" provides a humorous and thought-provoking take on the complexities of high school life.

BREAKFAST CLUB

"The Breakfast Club" is a quintessential '80s film directed by John Hughes. It brings together a diverse group of high school students in Saturday detention, transcending stereotypes and forming unexpected connections. The film delves into the complexities of teenage life and remains a timeless coming-of-age classic that continues to resonate with audiences. "The Breakfast Club" is an enduring exploration of the stereotypes that define high school students and their capacity to break down those barriers.

LOL

"LOL" features Miley Cyrus as a teenager navigating the challenges of love and friendship. While it may not reach the heights of some other teen dramas, it provides an entertaining look at the ups and downs of adolescent relationships. "LOL" offers a relatable portrayal of the complexities of teenage romance and the friendships that shape one's youth.

KAPOOR AND SONS

"Kapoor and Sons" is a heartwarming Bollywood film that explores family dynamics and secrets. With a talented ensemble cast and a mix of humor and drama, the movie delves into the complexities of familial relationships. It's a poignant and relatable exploration of love and forgiveness within a dysfunctional family. "Kapoor and Sons" provides a touching and emotionally resonant portrayal of the intricacies that define family relationships.

DEAR ZINDAGI

"Dear Zindagi" is a groundbreaking Bollywood film that treats therapy and psychology with seriousness, a departure from traditional Indian cinema. The movie delves deep into the journey of self-discovery and the art of letting go of the past. Alia Bhatt's remarkable performance as a young woman seeking answers through therapy sessions with Shah Rukh Khan is emotionally resonant. The film offers a refreshing exploration of personal growth, self-acceptance, and the importance of mental well-being. "Dear Zindagi" is a must-watch for those seeking a thought-provoking and insightful cinematic experience.

WAKE UP SID

"Wake Up Sid" is a coming-of-age Bollywood film that follows the journey of a young man discovering his passion and independence. It features a heartfelt performance by Ranbir Kapoor and beautifully captures the essence of youth and self-discovery. The film's relatable story and engaging characters make it a delightful watch for those on the cusp of adulthood. "Wake Up Sid" is a heartwarming coming-of-age tale that beautifully portrays the journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's dreams.

COMEDY

GROWN UPS

"Grown Ups" is a hilarious comedy that brings together a star-studded cast, including Adam Sandler, Chris Rock, and Kevin James, as childhood friends reuniting for a weekend getaway. The film is packed with witty humor, relatable family dynamics, and unforgettable moments, making it a feel-good comedy that provides plenty of laughs and heartwarming moments.

THAT'S MY BOY

"Thatâ€™s My Boy" is a raucous comedy starring Adam Sandler and Andy Samberg as a father-son duo with a tumultuous relationship. The film offers a mix of crude humor and outrageous situations, providing ample laughs for those who appreciate Sandler's signature style of comedy.

MURDER MYSTREY

"Murder Mystery" pairs Adam Sandler with Jennifer Aniston in a comedy-mystery that takes them on a whirlwind adventure during a European vacation. The film offers a lighthearted and entertaining take on the classic whodunit genre, combining humor and suspense for an enjoyable cinematic experience.

HEY BABY

"Heyy Babyy" is a Bollywood comedy featuring Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, and Fardeen Khan as three bachelors who suddenly find themselves taking care of a baby. The film combines slapstick humor with heartwarming moments, providing an entertaining mix of laughter and emotion.

PYAAR KA PUNCHNAMA 2

"Pyar Ka Punchnama 2" is a Bollywood comedy that humorously explores the complexities of modern relationships. The film follows the lives of three young couples dealing with the ups and downs of love and commitment, offering relatable humor and sharp wit that resonates with the audience.

3 IDIOTS

"3 Idiots" is a standout on this list, not just as the last entry but also as one of the best. While it's undeniably hilarious, the film goes much deeper. It tackles profound matters of life, work, and passion. As it unravels the journey of three friends through the pressures of the education system and societal expectations, it strikes a chord with the youth of India. "3 Idiots" beautifully highlights the importance of following one's passion and dreams, a message that resonates with those who often find themselves on a conventional path. It's a must-watch for its comedy and its inspirational depth.
